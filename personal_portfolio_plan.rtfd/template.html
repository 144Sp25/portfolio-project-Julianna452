Hello! Welcome to Julianna's Portfolio!

Julianna Lawrence is a 21-year-old Graphic Designer from Kansas City, Kansas. She has always had a passion in creativity and spent most of her time in art classrooms.

Julianna has worked for the Northwest Missourian for over 3 years and does freelance graphic design projects on the side. She has also taken many scholarship positions, such as a Social Media internship with the School of communications and Mass Media.

Her main goal is to creatve an image for her clients and bring to life what they have ideas for but have trouble putting together.
